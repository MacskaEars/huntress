# How to request a feature

This is about the same amount of work as sending an email for you, but making requests or reporting issues publicly helps everyone more.

1. Click "Issues" above, just to the right of "<> Code"
  - ![Click "Issues" above, just to the right of "<> Code"](https://github.com/MacskaEars/huntress/blob/main/request/1.png)
3. Click "New issue" in the top right
  - ![Click "New issue" in the top right](https://github.com/MacskaEars/huntress/blob/main/request/2.png)
4. Fill out the text boxes and submit!
  - ![Fill out the text boxes, you'll need a title and content](https://github.com/MacskaEars/huntress/blob/main/request/3.png)
  - ![Here's what I entered](https://github.com/MacskaEars/huntress/blob/main/request/4.png)
